Long-term national residence visa/Student visa/D4 visa required documentation

  1. filled -up all application forms (appointment confirmation, Visa Application form, SEF form)
  2. Valis passport
  3. passport size 2 copy photo (35*40 )

  5. Police clearance Certificate
  6. Proof of Accommodation letter (3/4 month)
  7. Bank paper applicant's name (20-30 lac BDT)
  8. Scholarship information if have
  9. Offer letter
  10. No Objection letter from the last Institute
  11. Attested last certificate and mark sheet main copy and 1 photocopy from Bangladesh foreign ministry
  12. Visa fee by cash, 7252 rupi or 90 Euro, if not have a scholarship, BFS Service Charge of INR 2648/-.
  13. one way valid flight booking copy, insurance date, and flight date would same date
